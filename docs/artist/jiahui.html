Jiahui Cai

Ectogenesis

Jiahui Cai is a Chinese interdisciplinary researcher in computational art. He holds an undergraduate degree in industrial design and his work in 2018 won the national second prize and Beijing grand prize in the National Industrial Design Innovation Competition. His current areas of research include the production of experimental musical instruments through physical computation, the use of algorithms to explore the possibilities of fractal forms, and the creation of Audio-Visual live performance inspired by the cybernetic control of groups through streaming media. He also uses machine learning to generate computational life forms, and this work was selected for The 1st Annual METAVERSE Art@VENICE.
