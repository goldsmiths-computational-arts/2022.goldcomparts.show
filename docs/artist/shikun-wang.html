• (SUB)SYSTEMS

Shikun Wang

iMmunity --- Cyber Relic

The future "exoskeleton" of human immunity.

The work explores the AI, medical and ethical challenges facing humanity in an age of strong intelligence, using a science fiction perspective and a grand scenario of future global epidemics. The work is a 'stolen immune system from the future' that acts as an exoskeleton to supplement and enhance the human immune system from the outside. In 2079, human immunity is generally taken over by artificial intelligence. Every week a new immune product is released, something that actually evolved from what was once a mobile phone .......

Shikun Wang is a young Chinese artist. He has a Fine Art background, is also an interdisciplinary researcher and practitioner. His main medium is installation, and his current practice involves artificial intelligence, ethics, medicine, sociology, and philosophy. "Sound visualization" is one of his former research topics.
