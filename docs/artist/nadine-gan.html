Nadine Gan

Come Pull My Heart Strings

Nadine Gan is a multidisciplinary Creative from Indonesia. With a background in Fashion Communication, it did not take her long to realize Computational Arts does not sit within her comfort zone. Through exploring different mediums such as physical computation and generative art, she has never questioned herself more. However, she has embraced her love-hate relationship with coding and aims to create a positive and entertaining experience from her computational work

An interactive sculptural installation

Just like today’s dating scene, this installation involves many strings attached that goes everywhere and nowhere. Feel lost yet empowered by being able to pull on my ‘heart strings’ and watch how (slow or fast) feelings can change on the screen.
