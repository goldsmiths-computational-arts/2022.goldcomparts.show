Chia-Yu Tsai

Regularity & Chaos

Chia-Yu Tsai is a creative technologist, user experience designer and new media artist with a background in graphic design. He combines aesthetics with logic, and perception with analysis, devoted to developing his creative practice with technologies. His works focus on creating complex forms with algorithms and rules and keep pushing the boundaries from various dimensions (e.g mathematics, machine learning, 3D modeling).
