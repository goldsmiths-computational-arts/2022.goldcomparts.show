a digital pet for managing chronic illness

I sculpt and bring to life my own digital symbiotic animals and plug them into my own endocrine system. Data harvested from a sub-dermal device measuring my blood glucose is communicated to me in a range of ways dependent on the companion animal chosen. Growing up with spore, pokemon and tamagotchi, I now create digital creatures that harness chronic illness as an interface and opportunity.
