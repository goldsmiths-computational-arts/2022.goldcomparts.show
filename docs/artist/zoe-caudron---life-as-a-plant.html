Zoe Caudron

Holders

3D printed vases made of shared stories

As individuals write and share stories or memories on the touch screen, new shapes are generated as pots, vases, containers. Holders. The amalgation of multiple stories creates ruptured or flowing shapes, made whole, and printed using wood filaments.

Zoe Caudron is a French artist and creative technologist. With a background in animation and visual effects, her practice now focuses on themes revolving around sustainability, individual narratives and anxiety. She uses both digital and physical mediums to convey her ideas, creating generative and interactive artworks. She also likes exploring connections between technology and biology, and how the two can be combined harmoniously and ethically.
