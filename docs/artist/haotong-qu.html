Look at the same thing from different sides

My project consists of two parts, the installation and the mapping The installation: on the left side is a card pool where people can draw their favourite cards and place them on the sensors on the right side to trigger different projections. mapping: the projection is made on the wall or on a cut out board.On the wall there is a screen where the project is presented.
