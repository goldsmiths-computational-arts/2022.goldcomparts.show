𝒞𝓎𝒷𝑜𝓇𝑔 𝒮𝒶𝓁𝓁𝓎 𝒶𝓃𝒹 𝓉𝒽𝑒 𝒯𝓌𝒾𝓃𝓈

Malu Laet is a sound and performance artist. Her work focuses in haptic technologies, speculative futures, interactive music. Her practice of sculpture, installation and performance engage with an intuitive mode of worldbuilding, where alternative worlds emerge from her making and are open for the viewers speculation. Her conceptual research is around new materialism and cosmotechniques conjoined with computational sound studies. She uses the methodology of theory-fiction to compose her texts. Her engagement with technologies seeks to develop haptic and sonic instruments from a speculative future. Her skill set is focused in Max Msp and physical computing.

𝒞𝓎𝒷𝑜𝓇𝑔 𝒮𝒶𝓁𝓁𝓎 and 𝓉𝒽𝑒 𝒯𝓌𝒾𝓃𝓈 stand in a magic circle, they are about to to start an alchemical work of sonic integration…

𝒞𝓎𝒷𝑜𝓇𝑔 𝒮𝒶𝓁𝓁𝓎 is on the sanctuary, together with 𝓉𝒽𝑒 𝒯𝓌𝒾𝓃𝓈. They are haptic-avatars, etherial-cybernetic beings that crossed virtuality back to the matter, to form a cult of post-internet alchemy. 𝒞𝓎𝒷𝑜𝓇𝑔 𝒮𝒶𝓁𝓁𝓎 is a cyber-witch, an alienated fem-bot. She practices the art of spherical mediology, Mediology is a junction of media and medium, a approximation between technological communication and mediunic intuition. Her alchemy are works of networking and integration within and through the mediological sphere. 𝒯𝒽𝑒 𝒯𝓌𝒾𝓃𝓈 are telepathic creatures. The are a embodied the alchemical symbol of the rebis, a conjoined hermaphrodite being, that represents a reconciliation of virtuality and matter. They are soul-partitions of the same, They have a decentralised brain, And joined nervous system. Together, they build upon the hybrid strata of metal and flesh to compose a haptic form of cyborg interaction. They stand in a magic circle about to to start an alchemical work of sonic initiation. In the circle there are transfer objects, or technologies of magic, machines that facilitate telepathic connections. They have Sallys haptic sonic apparatus, some magnetised totems from the future, and wet-motor portal in the centre of the altar: all functioning to unveil the inner-magnetic flows between the machine end being.
