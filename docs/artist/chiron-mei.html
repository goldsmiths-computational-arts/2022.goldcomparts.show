Chiron Mei is a fartist born and raised on the Internet but is currently transforming into a pony with mullet hair. He is deadly obsessed with seeking natural forms and criticising everything felt unnatural. He often expresses his work by means of sinuous twists and turns, although his own thoughts are often entwined from time to time. Now he is experimenting with multi-disciplinary means of expression, although this is partly for making a living.

A seeming satire of crypto artworks

What do NFTs bring us? A revolutionary era of emerging art forms? A prosperous art trading market? Metaverse? Stop bullshitting. The fartist just got fed up with this discourse. He considers the core of crypto stuff as ownership declarations, which makes this form of artwork the dullest in history, and then this one came out. This machine spins, flings out liquid and can't wait to swagger, demonstrating its aspiration and ambition by means of metaphor.
