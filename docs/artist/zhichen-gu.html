Zhichen Gu

The Bad USBs

The USBs keys gonna be "bad" and make a mess

This project makes the assumption that the USB keys are initially "evil minds" who cause chaos. The keys will be handled incorrectly and have their data and information altered.

Zhichen Gu is a computational artist from China. He majors in architecture and environmental design and works in various sectors. His artistic inspiration comes from the modern and traditional clash brought about by scientific and technical advances. During his undergraduate studies, he worked with individuals from several disciplines, which strengthened his interdisciplinary thinking and solidified his desire to concentrate on digital interactions. His most recent research made use of computational arts, which blend physical computing with creative coding.
