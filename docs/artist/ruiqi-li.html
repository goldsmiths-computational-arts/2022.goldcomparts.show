pink butcher's

pink butcher's shop displays what she found on "her" journey of escaping the factory.

This is a multimedia work combining 3D visual/ moving installation/ sculpture telling the story of a female doll trying to find her body parts to escape the factory. Those body parts hanging on steel frames and layers of boxes which refers to the journey from mini assembly line to trolley products in butcher's shop but never herself.
