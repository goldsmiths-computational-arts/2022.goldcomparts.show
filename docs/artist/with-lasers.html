with-lasers is a computational artist, who seeks to leverage their computational background to research, (re)discover and create new works that explore computation and its features. By looking at early techniques in computer graphics, they seek to imagine worlds in which the computing power available to the modern artist, can be applied to these often abstract visual techniques, creating new and intricate worlds that sit at a juncture of conventional spaces, three dimensional, but not three dimensional, complex yet basic. Producing works in a variety of media, from prints, to films, to performances that look to understand how instruments of both sound and video can be created.
