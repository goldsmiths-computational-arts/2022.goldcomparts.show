• (SUB)SYSTEMS

Liwen Wang

Reports

Some random 3d shapes generated based on weather and real time air quality in different cities, a data visualization work.

This piece is an exploration of real data visualization, importing data as an attribute value into a graph to generate some organic shapes. This work explores the relationship between economic conditions and air pollution through 3d data visualisation.

Liwen Wang usually called by Rose Wang, is a designer specializing in 3d designs, with a background in digital media. Currently moved into the field of computational and interactive art. Starting generate artworks by code and trying to fuse them with 3d method to explore new artistic possibilities.
