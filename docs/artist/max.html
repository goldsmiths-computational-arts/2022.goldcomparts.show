Max Jala

Stonehench

Max is a visual artist and programmer specialising in game art and interactive installations. His recent work in simulations utilise humour and incongruity to create bizarre fictional worlds. While recent AI art is heavily driven by complex and highly intelligent learning algorithms, Max takes a different approach, incorporating primitive game AIs within a chaotic system to create moments of surprise and emergent behaviours.

Stupid and lazy AI battle adversity to create art

Stonehench is a simulation depicting strong but stupid AIs on their mission to create art. These AIs stack rocks based on a user's drawn instruction; however, this is no easy task as they battle various hinderances such as laziness, extreme weather conditions and death in their pursuit of artistic fulfillment.
