Wanxiao Chen is a multidisciplinary artist. Her exploration of self and critique of social events inspire her creative work. Her interdisciplinary background in film and computational arts enables her artworks to be presented in a variety of media such as installation, games, and performance, as well as to express themes through a narrative approach.Through her work she hopes to create connections with people and to reflect on the current social situation together.
